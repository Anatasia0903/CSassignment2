About Me

Hello, my name is Thu Dong and I have been dancing since I can remember. My passion for dance led me to become a professional performer before I started primary school. Dancing has been such a huge part of my life that I can hardly imagine where I would be without it. In all my years of performing, my love for visual art, especially stage and costume design, has only continued to grow. While performers like myself often receive the spotlight, I have always been more inspired by the dedication and hard work of those behind the scenes. Stage and costume designers work tirelessly and put in long hours in order to create the perfect atmosphere for a performance. They are the unsung heroes of any production, often going unnoticed and unappreciated. Their skill and knowledge in various fields never ceases to amaze me, which has led me to aspire to become the next crucial crew member behind the stage. My ultimate goal is to work alongside these talented individuals and to bring my own creative vision to their expertise. I am excited about the opportunities that await me and I'm looking forward to contributing to the world of visual art. Thank you for taking the time to read a little bit about me.

My Services

Performing

As a trained and graduated professional ballet-contemporary dancer, I also proudly dance Vietnam traditional dance as it is a part of my heritage. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge by studying Jazz and Afro dance in an academic research approach as well as a practical aspect to understand the effects of globalization and the expansion of African-American culture in Vietnam.

Teaching

Inspired by my mother, who taught me to strive to be a good teacher, no matter what career I choose.
